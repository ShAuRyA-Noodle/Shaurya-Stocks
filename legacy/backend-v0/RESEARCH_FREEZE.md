## Research Freeze Declaration

The core intelligence system has been validated using:
- Walk-forward evaluation
- Regime-aware execution
- Confidence calibration
- Stability and risk controls

Performance is intentionally conservative with low drawdown,
demonstrating realistic, leakage-free behavior.

Further alpha research is out of scope for this project.
The system is now treated as a decision-support engine,
not a profit-maximization tool.
